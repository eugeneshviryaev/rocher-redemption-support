# Rocher Redemption — Support

Thanks for playing. This is a small game built by a small team
in our spare time, but we read every message.

## Get in touch

- **Email:** eugene.shviryaev@gmail.com
- **Bugs, crashes, weird behavior:** include your iPhone model
  and iOS version if you can. Screenshots help.
- **Feature requests, balance feedback, "this character should
  exist":** all welcome.

## Known limitations

- iPhone only, portrait only
- Requires iOS 17 or later
- Test ad units may show "Test Ad" overlays during this beta —
  this is expected

## About

Rocher Redemption is a roguelite tribute to our team's annual
Ferrero Rocher eating competition. Every character is based on 
a real teammate. Some have requested anonymity. None of them 
got it. If your likeness feels off, email — happy to regenerate.



## Privacy

The game stores your career progress locally on your device.
No personal information is collected by us. Google Mobile Ads
powers the optional Water Break mechanic — see Google's Mobile
Ads SDK privacy disclosures for details on what they collect:
https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903
